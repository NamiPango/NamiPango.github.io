Teaching

Topics Delivered (Internal Seminars/Workshops)

  • Data foundations for orthopedic nursing (data silos, lineage, metadata, coding systems)
  • Dashboard literacy for VBHC and NSO monitoring
  • Practical PROMs (e.g., HOOS-PS) capture and clinic workflow integration

Teaching Philosophy

I teach with a “clinic-first, data-always” mindset: start from patient pathways and nursing reality, then layer standards, quality rules, and analytics. Clarity, reproducibility, and minimal burden to the bedside are my core principles.
