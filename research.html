Research

Areas of Interest

  1. Value-Based Orthopedic Care (VBHC)
    Outcomes and cost-effectiveness with an emphasis on PROMs (e.g., HOOS-PS, EQ-5D-5L), LOS, SSI, and patient experience.
  2. Clinical Data Engineering for Nursing
    Data lineage, standard terminologies (ICD-10, ICD-9, SNOMED CT, LOINC, RxNorm), auditability, and governance for nurse-led workflows.
  3. Smart Clinical Pathways & Alerts
    ERAS and spine pathways, post-operative follow-ups, and event-driven notifications integrated with hospital processes.
  4. Operational Dashboards & Quality Improvement
    NSO, process adherence, and safety metrics, supporting PDCA and VBHC leadership reporting across BDMS sites.

Methods & Tools

  • Workflow analysis, data dictionary creation, registry field curation
  • Google Workspace/Apps Script, EMR/PACS data mapping (conceptual), Power BI
  • Basic statistics and descriptive analytics for baseline overviews

Current Projects

  • CoE Ortho VBHC Dashboard (BDMS network)
  • Orthopedic Registry enablement (field design, mapping, PROMs onboarding)
  • Follow-up reminder engine for post-surgery intervals (2w, 6w, 3m, 6m, 1y)
