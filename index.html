Orthopedic Nurse Coordinator & Informatics Liaison

I am an Orthopedic Nurse Coordinator working at the intersection of clinical care and digital systems. I design and steward data workflows, dashboards, and registries for the BDMS Orthopedic Center of Excellence (CoE Ortho), aligning with Value-Based Healthcare (VBHC). My work bridges nursing, surgeons, and IT—turning frontline needs into robust, auditable data pipelines and practical tools.
